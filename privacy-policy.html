Renewly Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 18, 2026

Renewly is developed and operated by GV ROI Labs. This policy explains what information Renewly collects, why it is used, and the choices available to users.

1. Information we collect

Renewly collects the name and email address provided for account management and authentication. It also stores subscription, renewal, category, note, amount, website, and reminder information that users choose to enter. Authentication credentials are processed by Supabase Auth. Renewly does not collect payment card numbers, bank login information, location, contacts, government identification numbers, or advertising identifiers.

2. How information is used

Account information is used to authenticate users and synchronize their Renewly data across devices. User-entered information is used to provide subscription tracking, spending summaries, backup features, and renewal reminders.

3. Notifications

Renewly can schedule renewal notifications locally on a user’s device. The current release does not register or store a remote push-notification token. Email reminder delivery is not active in this release.

4. Storage and security

Renewly stores account and app data using Supabase. Database Row Level Security restricts each user to their own records. Data is transmitted using HTTPS/TLS. Authentication sessions are stored using the Android platform’s protected credential storage.

5. Service providers

Renewly uses Supabase for authentication and database hosting and Expo application components for building and operating the Android app. These providers process information only as necessary to deliver the service.

6. Advertising, analytics, and selling data

Renewly does not contain advertising or third-party tracking and analytics SDKs. GV ROI Labs does not sell, rent, or trade personal information and does not use subscription data for advertising or profiling.

7. Data choices

Users can add, edit, or delete subscriptions and reminders from inside Renewly. Users can export their app data from Settings. Users can permanently delete their account and associated data from Settings → Delete account.

8. Account deletion and retention

Deleting an account removes the authentication record and associated profile, subscriptions, custom categories, reminders, settings, preferences, and audit history from the production database. Minimal copies may remain temporarily in encrypted backups until standard backup rotation completes. See the Account Deletion page for instructions.

9. Children’s privacy

Renewly is intended for adults and is not directed to children. GV ROI Labs does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes materially, the updated date will be revised and users may be notified within the app where appropriate.

11. Contact

Privacy and support questions can be sent to Gurjan Oberoi / GV ROI Labs at gvroilabs@gmail.com.
