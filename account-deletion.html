Delete your Renewly account

Renewly is developed by GV ROI Labs.

Delete inside the app

  1. Open Renewly and sign in.
  2. Open Settings.
  3. Select Delete account and confirm the permanent deletion.

This removes the user’s authentication account and associated profile, subscriptions, custom categories, reminders, notification settings, preferences, and audit history.

Request deletion without the app

If you cannot access Renewly, email GV ROI Labs from the email address connected to your account. Use the subject “Renewly account deletion request.” We may request reasonable verification to protect the account before processing deletion.

Request account deletion

Data deleted

The authentication account, profile, subscriptions, user-created categories, reminders, notification settings, preferences, and associated audit history are deleted.

Retention

Account data is removed from the production database after verified deletion. Minimal copies may remain temporarily in encrypted backups until those backups rotate. Account deletion cannot be undone.
